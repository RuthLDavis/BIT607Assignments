Welcome to the Haukai Restaurant

The Haukai Restaurant is located in charming Kerikeri, part of the idyllic Bay of Islands, and only a 30 minute drive from Paihia and historic Waitangi. Come dine with us after a day of exploring the region.

We specialise in Maori kai (food) and providing a Te Ao Maori dining experience. We use a traditional karakia (blessing) to bless all the locally sourced food used in our kitchen.

We also provide Pakeha (non-Maori) menu options as well.
